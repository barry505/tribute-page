Dale Earnhardt

Seven time Nascar Cup Champion!

Timeline of Dale Earnhardt's Life:

  • 1951 - Born in Kannapolis, North Carolina
  • 1975 - Started Cup Career
  • 1979 - First Win - Southern 500 - Bristol
  • 1980 - 1st Cup Championship
  • 1986 - 2nd Cup Championship
  • 1987 - 3rd Cup Championship
  • 1990 - 4th Cup Championship
  • 1991 - 5th Cup Championship
  • 1993 - 6th Cup Championship
  • 1994 - 7th Cup Championship
  • 2000 - Last Win - Winston 500 - Talladega
  • 2001 - Death at Daytona 500

"It seems like I was put here to drive a race car. I was bred and raised to do that." Dale Earnhardt
